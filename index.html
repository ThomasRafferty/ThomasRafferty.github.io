Rafferty's Rookie Year of Gardening

Creation Story

I use to water my boss's garden at my old job, yet had never done any gardening myself. Upon moving to Brooklyn I decided once the spring rolled around I would give it a shot. I have really enjoyed it as a hobby, particularly to take my mind off the tumultuous times we currently reside. I built the box myself with my brother's power drill and supplies from home depot. The joys of eating something I have grown myself have be wonderful.
